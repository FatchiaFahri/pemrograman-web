About Us

Our shop was founded in 2016 and initially only available in 1 branch. As the coffee business has grown, we have had several branches. We ensure the quality of each of our branches is of the same quality as the center.

Asteroid coworking is here to be a comfortable place for those of you who want to finish your work while enjoying your favorite menu that might treat your bad mood.

We combine customer service with a wonderful, attractive place of work so we can promise you a serviced office environment that is like none other in the World.

Asteroid is workspace that will bringing joy in the future to the community in the world.
